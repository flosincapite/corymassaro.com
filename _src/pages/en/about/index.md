I make things with words and with code, sometimes both at the same time.
I like to learn languages, practice martial arts, and explore new places.
My writing focuses on technology and its unfortunate marriage to capital.

It would be appropriate to describe me as any of the following:

- Linux Luddite
- Silicon Valley apostate
- lapsed Classicist
- some sort of anarchist
- FOSS enthusiast
- metered poet

You can find my fiction, essays, and verse at Wrath-Bearing Tree,
The Wild Word, and Rattle.
I publish regularly on [Substack](https://substack.com/@corymassaro).
